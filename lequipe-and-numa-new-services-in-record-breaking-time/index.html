L’EQUIPE & NUMA - New services in record-breaking time

AMBITION

In the context of questioning its future business model, L’Equipe envisioned to leverage the 20th Soccer World Cup in Brazil and its archives to provide new content based on these data.

ACTION

In collaboration with NUMA, we develop a program in 2 phases: –a 48 hours hackathon to help emerge and prototype new ways and means to give coverage to the worldwide event; –followed by a 5 days bootcamp to jump from the prototype to a ready-to-go-to-market product.

IMPACT

The program ended up with 2 finalized products Mundial Memories and Mythe ou Mytho. In addition, L’Equipe is working with us on putting in place new short track program involving external stakeholders and they engage a restructuration of their databases.
